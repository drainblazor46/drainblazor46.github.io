Drawings

You're wondering how this technology is even possible and I'm sure its impossible in todays era. But in the future, we will be able to have millions of satellites hooked up with cameras that will be able to capture all the areas of the galaxy. The satellites that would be used would be made out of the most durable material as well as the camera as well that would capture 20k footage of each and every single happening in space. All of the satellites' signals will be boosted up to unimaginable ranges that are reachable even when they are several lightyears away from earth. The satellites are also able to avoid danger such as blackholes and are mostly going to film at a safe distance to capture all the footage in real time.
