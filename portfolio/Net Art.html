This game takes place in a world where Pokemon are meant to be captured by humans in order to serve their bidding. The story follows Ash's first steps of becoming a Pokémon trainer(or not) where your actions have consequences. Ash has a dream of becoming the Pokémon master in his small town known as Pallet Town. It is the starting area where you will end up to start your Journey. As you progress you will meet many New Pokémon along the way that will either die or live in your choices as well as meet many different types of trainers.
