Immense Competition

Video Description

My final project focuses on a young martial artist that has a rough time making a name for himself amongst the many other fighting styles out in the world. He decides to compete in a tournament to gauge how much he needs to improve his craft. Meanwhile, a figure in the crowd observes his skills from afar, silently judging his every move
