It was great because there was so much content to be enjoyed. I also was a big fan of the animated series of Kirby: Right Back At Ya! which was considered to be one of my favorite Saturday Morning cartoons. It was cool to see all of his different forms that he would accumulate throughout the games to be fully realized in an anime so that was pretty awesome.
